Our Mission

Capabilities

What AnalystAI Can Do

A comprehensive suite of analysis tools, all running in your browser.

Smart Data Ingestion

Parses CSV, Excel, and JSON files. Auto-detects column types, flags missing values, and assesses data quality.

Statistical Engine

Computes mean, median, mode, standard deviation, variance, correlations, and more — all in pure JavaScript.

Interactive Visualizations

Auto-generates bar charts, line charts, pie charts, scatter plots, histograms, and forecast charts.

Analysis Types

Four Powerful Analysis Engines

Each engine provides a different lens to understand your data.

📊

Descriptive Analysis

Answers: "What happened?"

Summary statistics, distributions, top categories, trend lines, and frequency analysis. The foundation of any data exploration.

🔍

Diagnostic Analysis

Answers: "Why did it happen?"

Pearson correlations, outlier detection via IQR, anomaly flagging, and root-cause narratives to explain patterns in your data.

🔮

Predictive Analysis

Answers: "What will happen?"

Linear regression, time-series trend projection, 5-period forecasts with confidence bands, R² scoring, and trend direction.

💡

Prescriptive Analysis

Answers: "What should we do?"

Top 5 ranked recommendations with action items, expected outcomes, priority levels, and estimated timelines.

Expert Modes

Data Analyst vs Business Analyst

Two specialized personas, each optimized for different workflows.

🔵 Data Analyst Mode

Focused on statistical analysis and data exploration.

  • Summary statistics (mean, median, mode, std dev)
  • Distribution histograms
  • Correlation analysis and heatmaps
  • Outlier detection (IQR method)
  • Linear regression forecasting
  • Trend analysis and projections
  • Data quality scoring
  • Interactive chart generation

🟢 Business Analyst Mode

Focused on strategic insights and business frameworks.

  • Everything in Data Analyst mode, plus:
  • SWOT Analysis generation
  • Risk identification matrix
  • Stakeholder impact mapping
  • Cost-benefit summaries
  • Business KPI dashboards
  • Strategic recommendations
  • Process optimization insights
