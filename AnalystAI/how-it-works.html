How AnalystAI Works

Three simple steps stand between you and professional-grade data insights.

The Process

From Raw Data to Expert Insights

1

Upload Your Dataset

Drag and drop or click to browse for your data file. AnalystAI supports multiple formats:

CSV Excel (.xlsx) JSON

Auto-detects column types (numeric, categorical, date, text)
Flags missing values and calculates data quality score
Shows instant data preview table (first 10 rows)
100% client-side — your data never leaves your browser

2

Select Your Role & Analysis Type

Choose your expert persona and what kind of analysis you need:

🔵 Data Analyst Mode

Statistics, distributions, correlations, trends, and forecasts

🟢 Business Analyst Mode

SWOT analysis, risk matrices, KPIs, and strategic recommendations

Pick one or more analysis types: Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive, or Prescriptive. Optionally ask a natural language question.

3

Get Instant Expert Insights

Hit "Run AI Analysis" and receive a complete analysis package:

AI Narrative Insights
Interactive Charts
KPI Dashboard
Recommendations
